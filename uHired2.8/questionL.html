Frequently asked questions:

1. Do I need to be a northwestern student to be able to apply for jobs?
Reply: Yes and no. There are some jobs which redirect you to the pages of the corresponding employers that posted it, for these jobs you don not need to login using your NetID and password (which are the login credentials for most people associated with northwestern university). However some jobs applications are through our website. In this case you will not be able to apply to those jobs.

2. Will your website work on my mobile device ?
Reply: We have not designed the site for cell phones yet. However you should have no issues seeing the website on devices with small displays (Cell phones, tablets, etc.)

3. How can I post a job?
Reply: It is easy. Create an account, fill up the basic job information and post it on our website. You do not need our permission to post jobs on our website. However, we will regularly review the job postings to avoid spam.

4. I am a student, how can I follow up with my job application?
Reply: We recommend you to directly follow up with the employer.

5. Is your website browser specific?
Reply: No. Our website is tested on works well on latest versions of Internet Explorer, Firefox, Chrome and Safari.

6. Can we get a replicate of your website for our university?
Reply: Sure! We charge a monthly fee of $3000 and a signup fee of $7000. The signup fee includes special customizations for your university. We believe in providing a seamless experience for our customers and we regularly tweak our website for best possible performance.

7. Do you allow ads on your website?
Reply: Of course. We charge a signup fees of $500. For every click from a unique user we charge $5.
